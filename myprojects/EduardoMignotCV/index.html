Introduction

I am an experienced, dynamic and results-driven Digital manager with a proven track record of delivering complex strategic and technical projects to high-growth companies including tech start-ups. I am passionate about innovative products and technology.

I have spent the last 7 years working in a variety of analytical and managerial positions across Europe in the Digital Sector. My experience in Tech and Analytics, as well as my interpersonal skills, allow me to understand complex issues and explain them in a simple manner.

I have a high commercial acumen, strong investigative and problem-solving capabilities, and excellent stakeholder management skills building strong relationships with people in the company, customers, and partners.

My positive mindset and good people skills make me a great team-player in fast-paced environments. I have a growth mindset: I am always thriving to improve myself and eager to learn new solutions for my future projects.

I enjoy the challenges associated with developing innovating business models in new markets and delivering strategic solutions driven by data.
